# KL Internal Policies

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. All remote work must be approved by the team lead. 
Video calls are mandatory for all team meetings. Internet speed requirement: minimum 50 Mbps.

## Vacation Policy
Full-time employees accrue 15 vacation days per year. Unused days can be rolled over up to 5 days.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance via the HR portal.

## Expense Reimbursement
Submit expense reports within 30 days. Receipts required for amounts over $25.
Common approvals: software subscriptions, home office equipment, business travel.
Manager approval required for expenses over $500.

## IT Security
Passwords must be changed every 90 days. Use company VPN when accessing internal systems remotely.
Report any suspicious emails immediately to it-security@acme.com.

## AI Tools Usage
Generative AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) may be used for work with client data only after anonymization.
Do not enter confidential customer information into public AI tools.
