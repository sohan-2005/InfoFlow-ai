1. Work Hours
Standard working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are allowed flexible arrival between 9:00 AM and 10:30 AM.

2. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to:
- 18 Paid Leave days annually
- 12 Sick Leave days annually
Unused paid leave can be carried forward up to 10 days.

Emergency leave requires immediate notification to HR.

3. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week.
Full-time remote work requires manager approval.

4. Code of Conduct
All employees must maintain professional behavior.
Harassment, discrimination, or data misuse will result in disciplinary action.

5. Data Security
Internal documents must not be shared externally.
Access credentials should never be shared.
All systems require two-factor authentication.
